I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the [Computer Science Department](https://cs.uiowa.edu/) at the [University of Iowa](https://uiowa.edu/), advised by [Peng Jiang](https://pengjiang-hpc.github.io/). I am also a member of the [IOWA-HPC Group](https://iowa-hpc.github.io/). Before coming to [UIowa](https://uiowa.edu/), I received an M.S. in Computer Science from the [University of Southern California](https://www.usc.edu/) and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from the [China University of Mining and Technology-Beijing](https://english.cumtb.edu.cn/).

My research lies in program optimization, with an emphasis on compiler optimization for graph mining and machine learning system.
