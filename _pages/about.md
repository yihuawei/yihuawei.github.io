I am a PhD student in the [Computer Science Department](https://cs.uiowa.edu/) at the  University of Iowa. I am a member of [IOWA-HPC Group](https://iowa-hpc.github.io/) and advised by [Peng Jiang](https://pengjiang-hpc.github.io/).

My research lies in program optimization, with an emphasis on compiler optimization for graph mining and machine learning system.
